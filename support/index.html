Common Questions

Does the app work offline?

Yes. Once installed, the entire book and all artwork are stored on your device. No internet connection is required to read.

Does the app collect any data?

No. Spirals of Kemush has no accounts, no analytics, and no server. Nothing leaves your device. See the Privacy Policy for full detail.

Can the app read aloud?

Yes. Tap the play button at the top of any panel to hear it read using your device's system voice. Note that native voices may mispronounce Klamath words; recorded narration in the author's voice is planned for a future update.

I'd like to support the author's work.

The book is available on Amazon: The Book of Spirals. The author's second novel, Captain Jack & The Original Renegades, is available in paperback.
