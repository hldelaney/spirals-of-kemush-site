2. Description of the App

Spirals of Kemush is an iOS reading application that presents the twelve origin myths of the Klamath Basin as written by H. L. Delaney. The App contains the complete text of The Book of Spirals (Basalt Sea Press, 2025) alongside accompanying panel artwork, and offers an optional text-to-speech read-aloud function via your device's built-in voices.

The App functions entirely offline. There is no account system and no server-side component.
